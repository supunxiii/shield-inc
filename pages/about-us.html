About Us

Who we are?

"Shield" was founded on July 4th, 1776, in the picturesque region of Bavaria, Germany, traces its origins to the visionary Adam Weishaupt. From its historic inception, Shield Inc. has been dedicated to safeguarding individuals, families, and businesses, creating a legacy of trust and protection that spans centuries. With a rich history rooted in commitment and a relentless pursuit of excellence, Shield Inc. has become synonymous with security and reliability, evolving to meet the ever-changing needs of its valued clients. As we embark on this journey through time, join us in exploring the remarkable story of Shield Inc., an insurance company deeply ingrained in the fabric of security and peace of mind.


Our Services

Home Insurance

Shield Inc's Home Insurance is your fortress of protection for your cherished abode. We offer comprehensive coverage to safeguard your home, belongings, and personal liability. Our policies provide peace of mind, ensuring that your investment remains secure even in the face of unforeseen events like fire, theft, or natural disasters.

Life Insurance

Shield Inc's Life Insurance is a promise of enduring security for your loved ones. We offer a range of life insurance options tailored to your unique needs. In the event of the unexpected, our policies ensure that your family is financially protected, allowing you to leave a lasting legacy of care and support.

Auto Insurance

With Shield Inc's Auto Insurance, your vehicles are protected on every journey. Our coverage goes beyond the basics, offering comprehensive protection for your car, as well as liability coverage for accidents. We're here to provide peace of mind and keep you on the road with confidence.

Health Insurance

Shield Inc's Health Insurance plans are designed to prioritize your well-being. We offer a range of health coverage options to ensure you have access to quality medical care when you need it most. Your health is our concern, and we're here to support you every step of the way.

Business Insurance

Shield Inc's Business Insurance is your partner in safeguarding your company's success. We understand the unique challenges businesses face, and our policies are tailored to protect your assets, employees, and operations. With our coverage, you can focus on growth, knowing that your business is secure.

Disability Insurance

Shield Inc's Disability Insurance provides a vital safety net when injury or illness disrupts your ability to work. Our policies offer financial support during challenging times, covering daily living expenses and medical bills. We're here to ensure that your financial stability remains intact, even when you can't work.
